Ecosystem

Token

DTN is a utility token that can be used for various purposes within the Designtralized ecosystem, such as staking, governance, and participation in the marketplace and contest systems.

Studio

Creative professionals to design, develop and deploy decentralized applications. It provides a user-friendly interface that enables the easy creation of contracts, Dapps, and tokens.

Repository

Repository is a decentralized file storage system designed to provide secure and efficient storage for digital files such as documents, images, videos, and more.

Management

Designtralized’s design resource management also includes features such as asset tracking, version control, and project management, allowing designers to keep track of the status of their resources and projects in real-time.

Marketplace

Marketplace is a platform that connects clients who need design services with professional designers who can provide high-quality designs. The marketplace is built on blockchain, which ensures that transactions are transparent and secure.

Contest

Contest is a that allows designers to participate in design contests hosted by individuals or businesses. These contests typically involve creating a design for a specific product or service, and winner of the contest is awarded.

Tools

Virtual

The virtual technology that enables creation and design of digital design and environments in a virtual space (metaverse).

Governance

Designtralized governance is based on a decentralized model, which means that the decision-making power is distributed among all token holders.

Roadmap

Phase 1

Launch Designtralized token (DTN) on Ethereum network and deploying bridge contract to integrate with other blockchains

Phase 2

Integrate DTN token into major Designtralized design tools, campaign and mass adoption into different design fields

Phase 3

Launch Designtralized Marketplace, where designers can sell their Designtralized-compatible smart contract designs to interested parties.

Phase 4

Launch of the Designtralized Studio, a powerful design tool for creating Designtralized-compatible smart contracts across different blockchains.

Phase 5

Rollout of the Designtralized Repository, a library location for hosting and sharing Designtralized-compatible smart contract designs.

Phase 6

Launch of the Designtralized Contest platform, which will enable designers to compete for rewards by creating the most innovative and useful Designtralized-compatible smart contracts.

Phase 7

Launch of the Designtralized Management platform, which will provide enhanced functionality for managing and tracking Designtralized-compatible smart contracts.

Phase 8

Rollout of the Designtralized Virtual Design tool, which will allow designers to test and refine their Designtralized-compatible smart contract designs in a virtual environment.

Phase 9

Integration of additional Designtralized-compatible smart contract design tools, expanding the ecosystem's capabilities and increasing the diversity of available designs.
