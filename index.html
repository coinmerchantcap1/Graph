Roadmap

Follow the Deszigntralized roadmap outlines our plan for advancing the ecosystem and achieving our vision of empowering designers, building a community, and driving innovation. Here are some key milestones we aim to achieve:

  • Sample Text

    Launch of the DFT Studio, a powerful design tool for creating DFT-compatible smart contracts.

    Q1 2023

  • Sample Text

    Introduction of the DFT Marketplace, where designers can sell their DFT-compatible smart contract designs to interested parties.

    Q2 2023

  • Sample Text

    Rollout of the DFT Repository, a centralized location for hosting and sharing DFT-compatible smart contract designs.

    Q3 2023

  • Sample Text

    Launch of the DFT Governance platform, which will allow DFT holders to vote on proposals related to the ecosystem's development.

    Q4 2023

  • Sample Text

    Introduction of the DFT Contest platform, which will enable designers to compete for rewards by creating the most innovative and useful DFT-compatible smart contracts.

    Q1 2024

  • Sample Text

    Rollout of the DFT Virtual Design tool, which will allow designers to test and refine their DFT-compatible smart contract designs in a virtual environment.

    Q2 2024

  • Sample Text

    Integration of additional DFT-compatible smart contract design tools, expanding the ecosystem's capabilities and increasing the diversity of available designs.

    Q3 2024

  • Sample Text

    Launch of the DFT Management platform, which will provide enhanced functionality for managing and tracking DFT-compatible smart contracts.

    Q4 2024
